Meet Sarah Bell Tini

An Early Start

Sarah grew up on a farm in Owen County, Indiana. Cooking was an early passion for her, learning how to make pizza and lasagna for the whole family from a young age. She had the advantage of working with mostly fresh-grown herbs year-round from her father's greenhouse.

The Joy of Cooking

Her enjoyment of cooking has only grown throughout the years, eventually including making fresh pasta. For years, she has honed her techniques, becoming legend to family and friends. Now she has found work to love in producing her pasta professionally for local restaurants, friends and family.
